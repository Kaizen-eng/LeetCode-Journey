## ✅ What I Learned

- Always solve the problem correctly before thinking about optimization.
- A sorted array often provides opportunities to avoid extra space or unnecessary work.
- Counting consecutive occurrences is enough when duplicate elements are grouped together.
- Looking for patterns in the problem statement can lead to elegant solutions.
